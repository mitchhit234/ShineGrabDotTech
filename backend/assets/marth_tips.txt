Avoid Marth's grab at low percent, especially on flatter stages and away from platforms, as his chaingrab is devastating.
Be calm off stage against Marth, his edgeguarding is exceptional, but he has to guess to cover your options. Mix up your choices.
Be careful attacking Marth's shield with unsafe aerials, as his shieldgrab is exceptional. Utilize shine grab to do effective shield pressure.
Utilize upair in combos against Marth to maximize damage dealt, to even the combo game.
